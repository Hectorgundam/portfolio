ItsInMyBacklog

Product Function Summary

The Job Application Tracker is an application designed to help job seekers manage and track their job applications in one centralized location. By integrating with Gmail, the application automatically extracts relevant information from job-related emails and organizes it in a user-friendly interface. This allows users to easily keep track of their job search progress, stay organized, and reduce the stress associated with managing multiple applications, that way they can focus more time on applying.

>

Problem Statement

Applying to jobs is a time-consuming and stressful process that can be overwhelming for job seekers. This becomes even more stressful when they factor in having to keep track of the applications, rejections, interviews, assessments, and offers that flood their email, making it difficult to stay organized and manage their job search effectively.
