ItsInMyBacklog

Collection and progress tracking platform designed for model kit builders to organize their backlog, tools, paints, and customization workflow.

Product Function Summary

This project aims to provide a comprehensive user-friendly solution for model kit collectors to manage their collections and track their customization progress.

Problem Statement

Model kit collectors often manage large backlogs and need a clearer way to track what they own, what they plan to build, and where each kit is in the customization process.
