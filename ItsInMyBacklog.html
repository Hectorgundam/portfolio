It's In My Backlog

A personal passion project where I'm developing a web application to help manage and track model kits and their customization progress. Eventually I would love for it to become a tool for model kit builders can use to help them manage their backlog, tools, paints, and keep track of their progress on each kit.

ItsInMyBacklog

Product Function Summary

This project aims to provide a comprehensive user-friendly solution for model kit collectors to manage their collections and track their customization progress.

Problem Statement

Model kit collectors usually have an inmense amount of model kits and they affectionately call it their backlog. Usually these start stacking and stacking to the point that they lose track of what kits they have in their backlog, what the plans were for that kit, and or what step they are in the build process.
